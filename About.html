About us

Square Eyes started a rainy afternoon of October 2022 in Oslo Norway. We at square eyes share a deep appreciation for “misunderstood” and low budget indie films. While we also want our community to share our love for these movies, we also want to engage with you. Thats why in 2023 we are starting the Square Eyes movie club! The movie club is for those of you who want to meet and discuss the movies with like minded people. You can also come with suggestions and much much more. Stay tuned! Our purpose with this site is to share some of the best indie films out there at the moment and get you to widen your perspective on what to watch.

If you have any questions or other inquiries, please use our contact form on the support page. We usually answer within 24H.
